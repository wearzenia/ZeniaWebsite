Common Questions

Things people ask us

Zenia is stocked at select women's clothing, ethnic wear, and nightwear retail stores across India. Our retail presence spans multiple states and is growing every month. If you're looking for Zenia in your city, reach out to us and we'll point you in the right direction.
Zenia is currently sold exclusively through retail stores. We believe comfort is something you feel before you buy — which is why we want every woman to experience the fabric in person. Keep an eye on our Instagram for updates.
We'd love to partner with you. Fill out the contact form above and select "Retailer / Store owner" — our team will get back to you within 1–2 business days with everything you need to become a Zenia stockist.
Zenia is designed for every woman. We offer an inclusive size range — because comfort should never be limited by size. Ask your nearest retailer for the full size range available in-store.
We work with seven carefully chosen fabrics — Rayon, Satin, 100% Cotton, Modal, Alpine, Linen Rayon, and Korean Silk. Each fabric is matched to the right product: for example, our nighties come in all seven fabric options, kaftans come in Satin and Rayon, and our feeding nighties use Alpine for extra softness. Every choice is made with one question: will she be comfortable?
Absolutely. Our kaftans, coord sets, and loungewear pieces are styled for all-day wear — comfortable enough to stay in at home, polished enough for guests, errands, and casual social occasions.
We're always open to meaningful collaborations — whether you're a content creator, influencer, stylist, designer, or a brand that shares our values. Write to us at hello@zenia.in or use the contact form above and select "Collaboration enquiry." Tell us about yourself and your vision, and we'll get back to you.
Yes. We are always looking for quality-driven fabric suppliers and manufacturing partners who share our commitment to comfort and craftsmanship. If you work in textiles or garment manufacturing and believe in what we stand for, we'd love to connect. Use the contact form and select "Manufacturer / Fabric supplier."
