Our Philosophy

Comfort is not a
luxury — it is a right.

Zenia was born from a simple belief: that hardworking women deserve to come home to clothing that embraces them. Not just any clothing — but something beautifully made, thoughtfully designed, and gentle on the skin.

We are not fast fashion. We are a brand that believes the hours you spend resting are as important as the hours you spend working. Which is why we obsess over every fabric, every stitch, every silhouette.

Our Materials

Fabrics chosen for how they feel, not just how they look.

01

Rayon

Our most versatile fabric — lightweight, silky-smooth, and beautifully draping. Used across kaftans, short kaftans, midis, and coord sets for effortless everyday comfort.

02

Satin

A touch of indulgence for the woman who deserves it. Our satin drapes luxuriously and feels smooth all night — used in nighties, kaftans, and night suits.

03

100% Cotton

Breathable, hypoallergenic, and perfect for India's warm nights. Our cotton is soft-washed for an extra gentle touch — used in nighties and coord sets.

04

Modal

Incredibly soft, naturally temperature-regulating, and gentle on skin. Modal is what clouds would feel like — featured in our premium nighty range.

05

Korean Silk & Linen Rayon

For the nights you want to feel truly special. Korean Silk brings lustrous softness; Linen Rayon adds cool, breathable texture — both exclusive to our nighty range.

06

Alpine

Soft, warm, and cosy — Alpine fabric is chosen for our nighties and feeding nighties, offering exceptional comfort for both everyday rest and the gentleness new mothers need.
